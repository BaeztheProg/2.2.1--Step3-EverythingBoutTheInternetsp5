Stay Safe on Social Media

Learn how to protect your privacy, stay secure, and be mindful of your online presence on various social media platforms.

Instagram Safety Tips

  • Set your account to private.
  • Enable two-factor authentication.
  • Avoid sharing personal information publicly.

Snapchat Safety Tips

  • Don’t share your Snapcode with strangers.
  • Limit who can contact you by adjusting your privacy settings.
  • Be cautious when using the Snap Map feature.

YouTube Safety Tips

  • Keep your account private until you're comfortable.
  • Report inappropriate content or users.
  • Use the platform’s safety features, like restricted mode.
